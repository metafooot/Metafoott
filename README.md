## The FOOT Points System

**FOOT points** are the lifeblood of the METAFOOT experience. They are designed to be earned through participation, not purchased. They reward activity, power gameplay, and give holders a voice in the future direction of the project.

### Total Supply
The total number of FOOT points that can ever exist is permanently fixed at **500,000,000**. No more can ever be created.

### How Points Are Distributed

| Allocation | % of Total | Amount | Purpose |
|------------|-------------|--------|---------|
| Daily Rewards (Early Access) | 10% | 50,000,000 | Distributed daily to the earliest community members |
| Pre‑sale (coming soon) | 25% | 125,000,000 | Early community funding |
| Development Team | 15% | 75,000,000 | Locked for 2–4 years with a 6‑month delay |
| Long‑Term Staking | 15% | 75,000,000 | Incentivises long‑term holding and network security |
| Exchange Support | 10% | 50,000,000 | For future trading and availability |
| Community Growth | 10% | 50,000,000 | Marketing and partnerships |
| Reserve Fund | 15% | 75,000,000 | Future development, buybacks, strategic needs |

### Where FOOT Points Get Their Value

Unlike points in a typical mobile game that exist only inside that title, FOOT points have deep utility woven into every part of the METAFOOT experience:

*   **Train your player:** Spend points to upgrade your avatar's six core skills – Speed, Shot, Power, Intelligence, Brilliance, Accuracy. The more you invest, the stronger your virtual footballer becomes.
*   **Enter tournaments:** Pay entry fees in points to compete for massive prize pools. Winning means earning even more.
*   **Tip and reward:** On the social feed, you can tip other users to reward great content and support the community.
*   **Boost your reach:** Reserve points to increase your daily visibility on the platform – no hidden algorithms, just transparent tiers that anyone can verify.
*   **Spend in the real world:** The planned FOOT Rewards Card will let you use points at METAFOOT‑branded physical stores, online shops, and for event tickets – no currency conversion needed.

### Scarcity Over Time

A portion of every transaction – tournament fees, player trades, card payments – is used to permanently remove points from the system. This steady reduction continues until the active supply reaches just **100 million points**, creating long‑term value for those who hold them.

### From Early Access to the Full Platform

During the Early Access Phase, all points are stored locally on your device. When the full platform launches, a one‑time snapshot will be taken of every user's balance. Those stored points will then be converted into the permanent, fully‑usable form. You claim them simply by logging in with your personal backup key. It's zero‑friction earning today, with full utility tomorrow.

## The Full METAFOOT Experience

The early access app you see right now is the foundation. Here's how the entire ecosystem will unfold.

### 🔐 Your Account, Your Rules

When you first open METAFOOT, you create a personal account. A 12‑word backup key is generated on your device using strong randomness. You set a pass‑phrase, and that backup key is encrypted and stored safely in your browser's local storage. A unique Account ID is derived from your backup key – this is your identity in the METAFOOT world.

You can log back in with your pass‑phrase, your Account ID plus pass‑phrase, a full encrypted backup file, or simply your backup key. Because the backup key is the ultimate master key, no one can lock you out or block you. You are the only custodian of your account. This is true self‑sovereignty.

### ⛏️ Daily Rewards

Every 24 hours, you can claim your daily FOOT points by tapping the glowing circle. The reward rate depends on the total number of active participants – a built‑in scaling mechanism that ensures fairness:

| Active Participants | Daily Reward |
|---------------------|--------------|
| 0 – 14,999          | 4 points     |
| 15,000 – 99,999     | 2 points     |
| 100,000 – 499,999   | 1 point      |
| 500,000 – 999,999   | 0.5 points   |
| 1,000,000+          | 0.25 points  |

This system rewards early believers while preserving value for everyone. The total daily rewards pool is capped at 50 million points. A live countdown timer shows you exactly when your next claim is ready.

### 🏋️ Player Training

Every user has a digital footballer with six core skills – Speed, Shot, Power, Intelligence, Brilliance, and Accuracy. You spend points to improve these stats. However, the cost per point doubles every 20 levels, creating a natural balance:

| Current Level | Cost per Point |
|---------------|----------------|
| 0 – 19        | 0.3 points     |
| 20 – 39       | 0.6 points     |
| 40 – 59       | 1.2 points     |
| 60 – 79       | 2.4 points     |
| 80 – 99       | 4.8 points     |

This progressive curve prevents anyone from buying instant dominance and rewards dedication over time. Your player's total "worth" is the sum of all points spent on training – a transparent valuation that will underpin the future player marketplace.

### 👥 Friend Referrals

Your Account ID is also your referral code. Share it with friends; when they enter your code, you both earn a reward. The reward amount scales with the participant growth, ensuring incentives remain fair as the network expands. Pending rewards are tracked in the app and will be credited when the full platform launches.

### 📱 Daily Tasks

Stay engaged and earn extra points by completing simple social tasks that refresh every 30 minutes:
*   Visit the official Telegram channel – earn 0.6 points.
*   Visit the official X (Twitter) account – earn 0.6 points.

Cooldown timers keep everything transparent, and the app uses deep links to open the respective apps smoothly.

### 📱 Social Feed (Coming Soon)

Imagine a social feed that works like the early days of Twitter – purely chronological, with no hidden algorithms. Your reach is determined by a transparent, point‑based tier system. The more points you hold, the more people see your posts, but everyone starts with a generous free allocation. No pay‑to‑boost, no shadow banning, no opaque curation. Just honest, community‑driven conversation about football.

### ⚔️ PvP Arena & Tournaments

Challenge other players to real‑time football matches. Wager points on the outcome – the winner takes the pot, minus a small ecosystem fee that goes towards the scarcity mechanism. Climb the seasonal leaderboard, earn exclusive badges, and prove your training regimen is the best.

Managers will be able to form teams of trained players and enter four‑tier leagues:
*   **FOOT Survival** – Entry level, top 3 advance.
*   **FOOT Mission League** – Premier league equivalent.
*   **FOOT Classic League** – International club competition.
*   **FOOT Elite League** – The ultimate championship.

Prize pools are split fairly: 70% to the players, 30% to the manager – rule enforced transparently.

### 🛒 Player Marketplace

Once the full game launches, a marketplace will allow managers to trade trained player avatars. The baseline value of each player is determined by the total points invested in their skills – a clear, fair valuation that gives early participants instant liquidity. Scout talent, build your dream team, and trade like a real football club.

### 💳 FOOT Rewards Card & Physical Shops

One of the most ambitious parts of the METAFOOT roadmap is the FOOT Rewards Card. It's a real‑world card funded directly by your points balance. Use it online, or walk into a METAFOOT‑branded retail store and pay with your phone. No currency exchange, no third‑party processor – just pure ecosystem utility. This closes the loop between the digital and physical worlds.

### 🎮 The Full Game

The full METAFOOT game will be a realistic 3D football manager experience, first on mobile and later on PC. The Early Access Phase is your head start – train your avatar now, collect points, and be ready to dominate from day one.
